Hotel Information

Paradise Hotel Busan

Address: 296 Haeundaehaebyeon-ro, Haeundae-gu, Busan, South Korea

Paradise Hotel Busan is a luxury beachfront hotel located directly on Haeundae Beach. It offers ocean views, premium rooms, spa facilities, restaurants, casino access, and convenient access to Busan’s major coastal attractions.

Nearby Attractions

Haeundae Beach

What: Busan’s most famous white-sand beach.

Why visit: Iconic ocean views, sunrise walks, festivals, and beachfront cafes.

Best time: Sunrise or sunset.

From hotel: 1–2 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 30 min – 2 hr.

Best for: family, couples, photography

Map

SEA LIFE Busan Aquarium

What: Underwater aquarium beneath Haeundae Beach.

Why visit: Sharks, rays, and tropical sea life; great for kids.

Best time: Late morning or rainy days.

From hotel: 3–5 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 1–2 hr.

Best for: family, kids

Map

Haeundae Traditional Market

What: Local market with street food and fresh produce.

Why visit: Try Korean snacks, hotteok, jeon, and seafood pancakes.

Best time: Late afternoon / early evening.

From hotel: 10–15 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 1–2 hr.

Best for: food, solo, family

Map

Dongbaekseom Island

What: Small forested island connected by walking paths.

Why visit: Coastal trail, camellia trees, lighthouse, ocean views.

Best time: Morning or late afternoon.

From hotel: 15–20 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 1–1.5 hr.

Best for: couples, photography, solo

Map

Nurimaru APEC House

What: Modern Korean-style pavilion that hosted the APEC summit.

Why visit: Architecture and panoramic ocean views from Dongbaekseom.

Best time: Daytime.

From hotel: 15–20 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 30–60 min.

Best for: couples, photography

Map

BUSAN X the SKY

What: One of Korea’s tallest observation decks at LCT Tower.

Why visit: 360° views of Haeundae, the ocean, and the city skyline.

Best time: Sunset & night.

From hotel: 5–10 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 1–2 hr.

Best for: couples, family, photography

Haeundae Blueline Park / Mipo Station

Why visit: Scenic coastal ride from Mipo to Cheongsapo / Songjeong.

Best time: Daytime, especially sunny weather.

From hotel: 15–20 minutes on foot.

Recommended duration: 1–2 hr.

Suggested Walking Routes

Route A — 1-hour Easy Walk

Paradise Hotel → Haeundae Beach → Haeundae Market → Return to hotel

Estimated time: ~1 hour

Difficulty: Easy (flat)

Best time of day: Late afternoon

Best for: First-time visitors, families

Route B — 2–3 Hour Coastal Route

Paradise Hotel → Mipo Station → Blueline Park → Cheongsapo → Skywalk

Estimated time: 2–3 hours

Difficulty: Easy – moderate

Best time of day: Morning or late afternoon

Best for: Couples, photography

Route C — Half-day Haeundae Highlights

Paradise Hotel → Dongbaekseom → Nurimaru APEC House → BUSAN X the SKY → Haeundae Market

Estimated time: ~4–5 hours

Difficulty: Moderate (some walking + stairs)

Best time of day: Late morning start

Best for: Sightseeing-focused visitors

Transportation

From Gimhae International Airport

Taxi: ~45–60 minutes depending on traffic.

Light rail + subway (Line 2) to Haeundae Station: ~70–90 minutes.

Airport limousine bus to Haeundae: ~60 minutes.

From Busan Station (KTX)

Taxi: ~25–40 minutes.

Subway: Line 1 → transfer to Line 2 at Seomyeon → Haeundae Station (~45–55 minutes).

By Subway

Take Busan Metro Line 2 to Haeundae Station, then ~10 minutes on foot to Paradise Hotel.

By Taxi

Show this address to the driver:

“파라다이스호텔 부산 — 부산광역시 해운대구 해운대해변로 296”

Local Transport Tips

Many sights are within walking distance. For longer trips, taxis are cheap and convenient.

Buy a T-money card at any convenience store for buses and subway.

Kakao T / Taxi Note

Download the Kakao T app to call taxis with English support.

You can pay by card or in cash.

Busan Metro Line 2 / Haeundae Station Note

Use Exit 5 or 7 from Haeundae Station and head toward the beach to reach Paradise Hotel.
